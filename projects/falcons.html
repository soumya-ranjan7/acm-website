Team Asailing Falcons


Assailing Falcons is the oldest and most profound Aero Design team in VIT Vellore. It's currently 6th in the world ranking according to SAE (Society of Automotive Engineers) Aero Design east. A set of multi talented engineers having varied skill sets, work impeccably hard together in creating the best radio controlled aircraft that they possibly can. Creating an absolute aeronautical masterpiece that combines state of the hour technology with adequate precision every year, is no easy task but the asailing Falcons keep excelling targets with absolute finesse every year. The ACM crew has been working tirelessly in creating an absolute seamless website to cater to their needs and to portray their excellence in the best way we know how, through amazing design and highly skilled web development.
